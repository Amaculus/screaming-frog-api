# Local SEO And Multi-Location Operations

## Multi-Location QA
- Crawl every location folder or subdomain into one comparable dataset.
- Detect missing location titles, missing localized headings, and duplicate metadata.
- Surface near-duplicate location pages with weak differentiation.
- Validate canonicals and indexability on city, region, and service-location combinations.

## Entity And Landing Page Governance
- Audit store, clinic, branch, or office pages for technical consistency.
- Detect orphaned location pages after IA or CMS changes.
- Track internal linking support from hub pages into local landing pages.
- Compare crawl-depth and inlink coverage across locations.

## Regionalization And Alternate Versions
- Validate hreflang and localized canonical relationships where location pages also vary by language.
- Detect regional template regressions before they spread across all markets.
- Build location-segmented monitoring for migrations, consolidations, or franchise rollouts.
- Produce per-region issue summaries for distributed teams.

## Operational Reporting
- Rank locations by technical risk rather than by alphabetical listing.
- Create recurring QA packs for market launches and sitewide template changes.
- Feed local landing page inventories into BI or reporting layers.
- Build exception lists for locations needing manual content or linking work.
