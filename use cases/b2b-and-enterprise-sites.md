# B2B And Enterprise Site Operations

## Complex Information Architecture
- Audit large service, solution, industry, and resource sections separately.
- Detect weak internal linking between demand-generation and solution pages.
- Track crawl-depth growth across sprawling enterprise navigation structures.
- Identify stale or duplicated hub pages created by matrixed site ownership.

## Governance Across Teams
- Compare crawl health by business unit or section owner.
- Separate platform regressions from local content-team mistakes.
- Build common QA checks for multiple site owners working in one CMS.
- Publish exception reports for teams responsible for specific sections.

## Lead-Gen Surface QA
- Protect pricing, demo, contact, and comparison pages with tighter crawl checks.
- Monitor redirects and canonicals on campaign and evergreen demand pages.
- Detect rendering changes that affect conversion-oriented templates.
- Track whether high-intent pages lose internal support over time.

## Platform Modernization
- Use crawl diffs to validate redesign and replatform rollouts.
- Measure whether old technical debt is being retired or reintroduced.
- Build standardized issue inventories before and after governance changes.
- Feed enterprise web-platform roadmaps with concrete defect concentration data.
