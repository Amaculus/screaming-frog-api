# Publishing And Programmatic SEO

## Editorial QA
- Audit article titles, descriptions, headings, canonicals, and indexability on every crawl.
- Find broken internal links inside article bodies and related-content widgets.
- Track author, tag, and archive pages that accumulate low-value or duplicate content.
- Detect JS-rendered metadata differences on complex publishing stacks.

## Programmatic Page Governance
- Monitor large generated page sets for thin content, weak differentiation, and duplicate signals.
- Compare semantic similarity across location, product, or query-template pages.
- Find pages that exist in the crawl but have no meaningful internal support.
- Detect title and meta templates that drift into obvious duplication.

## News And Discover Readiness
- Validate structured data, canonical consistency, and internal linking on article templates.
- Surface soft technical regressions before they affect crawlability or eligibility.
- Track template-level changes that silently affect hundreds or thousands of editorial pages.
- Build daily checks for recently published URLs only.

## Archive And Taxonomy Hygiene
- Separate evergreen content hubs from noisy archive or filter pages.
- Audit pagination, prev/next remnants, and crawl-depth growth inside archives.
- Flag taxonomy pages that absorb too many internal links relative to target content.
- Monitor internal link decay to high-value evergreen URLs over time.
