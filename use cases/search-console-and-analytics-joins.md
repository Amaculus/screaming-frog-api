# Search Console And Analytics Joins

## Search Console Enrichment
- Join crawl URLs with Search Console clicks, impressions, and index coverage states.
- Find indexable URLs with impressions but weak internal support.
- Detect non-indexable URLs still carrying search demand.
- Prioritize fixes by combining crawl defects with actual search exposure.

## Analytics Joins
- Combine crawl data with sessions, conversions, or revenue by URL.
- Rank technical issues by business impact instead of pure URL count.
- Separate issues on low-value pages from regressions on critical landing pages.
- Build section-level health views that blend technical and business metrics.

## Opportunity Models
- Find pages with traffic but weak metadata or poor internal support.
- Find pages with strong internal support but weak discoverability or indexing.
- Build remediation queues that combine demand, revenue, and technical severity.
- Detect content sections where crawl debt is concentrated on high-value templates.

## Reporting And Experimentation
- Use joined datasets for weekly optimization programs.
- Track whether fixes change both crawl quality and business metrics over time.
- Build dashboards that move from crawl-level defects to outcome-level impact.
- Support prioritization conversations with evidence instead of intuition.
