# Ecommerce And Marketplaces

## Merchandising And Template QA
- Audit product, category, brand, and search-result templates separately.
- Detect missing product titles, missing descriptions, or inconsistent heading patterns at scale.
- Track faceted URL growth, parameter sprawl, and duplicate content risk.
- Flag pagination and canonical errors in category flows before they affect crawl budget.

## Product Availability And Indexability
- Find product URLs returning 200 but blocked, noindexed, canonicalized away, or orphaned.
- Detect discontinued products still receiving internal links.
- Audit redirect behavior for out-of-stock or merged products.
- Verify product detail pages still receive internal links after navigation or CMS changes.

## Image And Performance Operations
- Find oversized image-heavy product pages and broken image references.
- Track missing size attributes and image-link issues on templates with heavy visual components.
- Audit mobile rendering and performance issue clusters across PDP templates.
- Build remediation queues for image, script, and layout inefficiencies.

## Marketplace Governance
- Segment crawl outputs by marketplace, storefront, region, or seller path.
- Compare duplicate and near-duplicate product content across storefronts.
- Validate hreflang and localized canonicals on regional product variants.
- Monitor search-result pages, filters, and parameter routes that should stay out of the index.
