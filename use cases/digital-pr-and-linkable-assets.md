# Digital PR And Linkable Asset Operations

## Linkable Asset QA
- Audit studies, tools, calculators, and campaign landing pages before outreach.
- Detect broken assets, weak metadata, or crawl barriers on pages intended to attract links.
- Validate indexability and canonical behavior on campaign pages with short release windows.
- Monitor whether supporting content properly links into flagship assets.

## Campaign Monitoring
- Track technical regressions on active campaign pages without reopening the GUI.
- Protect campaign URLs from redirect, noindex, or canonical mistakes during launch cycles.
- Diff campaign pages between launch and post-launch states.
- Build section-specific alerts for high-visibility assets.

## Internal Link Support
- Measure whether earned-link targets actually receive internal reinforcement.
- Find underlinked assets that should distribute authority into commercial sections.
- Validate hub-and-spoke support around campaign content.
- Detect when navigation or IA changes weaken asset discoverability.

## Reporting
- Package technical QA with outreach or campaign reporting.
- Provide evidence for whether technical issues limited a campaign surface.
- Build repeatable preflight and postflight checklists for launch teams.
- Preserve one crawl-backed record of every campaign rollout.
